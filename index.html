Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the questions we hear most from patients.

The standard recommendation is a check-up and professional cleaning every six months. Patients with a history of gum disease, frequent cavities, or other ongoing conditions may benefit from quarterly visits. Your dentist will advise the right schedule for your situation during your first consultation.

Professional teeth whitening is safe for most adults with healthy teeth and gums. It is not recommended for pregnant women, children under 16, or patients with extensive restorations on front teeth. A pre-whitening assessment is always performed to ensure the treatment is appropriate and to minimize sensitivity.

Dental implants, when properly cared for, can last a lifetime. The titanium post that integrates into the jawbone is designed to be permanent. The crown placed on top typically lasts 10 to 20 years before it may need replacement due to normal wear. Good oral hygiene and regular check-ups are essential to implant longevity.
